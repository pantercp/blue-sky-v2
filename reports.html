Check-in Inventory

The Check-in Inventory marks the initiation of thorough property inspection. During this crucial process, we conduct a comprehensive assessment of your property, capturing detailed photos and gathering data ranging from the make, model, and serial numbers of your appliances to essential building information. No detail is too small - scuffs, scratches on walls, and other items are diligently recorded.

Why is this important? The Check-in Inventory serves as the foundation for assessing deposit deductions when tenants vacate the property. Our meticulous documentation ensures a transparent and fair evaluation, providing both landlords and tenants with a clear record of the property's condition at the start of the tenancy.

Room Inspections for HMOs

In response to customer demand, we now offer individual room inspections, catering to the unique requirements of Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs). This service is particularly beneficial for properties where tenants frequently transition in and out of their rooms..

Check-Out Inventory

The Check-Out Inspection is our comprehensive revisit to your property, aimed at capturing detailed pictures and notes to depict its current state. We meticulously document any changes we observe and bring them to your attention promptly.

During this process, we identify items that have experienced fair wear and tear, ensuring a fair assessment. Additionally, we highlight any aspects where we believe deductions may be justified. Our goal is to provide you with a transparent account of the property's condition at the end of the tenancy.
